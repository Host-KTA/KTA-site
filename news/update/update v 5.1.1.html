update v 5.1.1 (2026.07.22 )

KanaTales Version v5.1.1 Update



1. Changes to Episode and Event Dungeon Rewards

The amount of Coins obtained from advertisement rewards has been increased.

Colored Ores will now be provided for each floor.



2. First Clear Rewards Added to All Dungeons

Talentium Marble rewards will now be provided upon the first clear of all dungeons.

In the Imperial Research Lab, first-clear rewards will be provided for clearing Buildings A through E according to the day of the week.



3. Clear Count Display Added to Each Dungeon

The number of clears will now be displayed at the top of each dungeon.



4. Changes to Dimensional Rift Opening Schedule and Rewards

The opening interval of Dimensional Rift has been changed.

Dimensional Rift will now open every 12 hours.

Normal Difficulty Reward : 10 Dimensional Fragments

Boss Difficulty Reward : 3 Skip Tickets

Daily Quest Reward (Once per day) : 1 Mileage



5. New Challenge Content: "Tower of Dimensions"

Three new towers, each consisting of 100 floors, have been added.

Tower Names : Tower of Summer, Tower of Children, Tower of Ocean

Each floor has Deck Point restrictions and rewards.

(Please refer to the Naver KanaTales Lounge for details.)



6. Trial Unique Card Enhancement System Added

A new enhancement system for Trial Unique Cards has been added.



7. Improvements to Character Card and Follower Card Enhancement

Character Cards will gain increased PvE effects when reaching Enhancement Level 5.

The Card Point cost for Follower Cards has been reduced.



8. Changes to Fight Normal Match Victory Rewards

(Please refer to the Naver KanaTales Lounge for details.)



9. Changes to Fight Ranked Match GP Rewards

(Please refer to the Naver KanaTales Lounge for details.)



10. Changes to Fight Ranked Match KP Acquisition Amounts

(Please refer to the Naver KanaTales Lounge for details.)



11. Fight Ranked Match Unlock Changes

When Fight Ranked Matches begin, all sections from Area A to Area E will be unlocked.



12. New Event Shadow Cards Added

13. Special Gacha System Improvements

The number of Pickup targets has been fixed to 1.

The Ensured Gauge has been changed to 100.

When the Pickup Plan is changed, the existing Ensured Gauge will be reset.

(Please refer to the Naver KanaTales Lounge for details.)



14. Special Card Pickup List

Unique card : Key of causality Sita(Vita)

Unique card : Traveler of Truth Asmis (Vita)



15. New Products Added

3 new Special Products have been added.

4 new Starter Products have been added.

(Please refer to the Naver KanaTales Lounge for details.)



16. Monthly Subscription Purchase Limit Changed

The maximum purchase limit has been changed from 1 to 6.

Additional purchases are only available when the remaining duration is 149 days or less.
